## Other rules

- Shot hit rate
  
  You can shoot if your aim aligns with certain letters. However, the probability of hitting a target varies. In other words, targets with a low hit rate will require multiple shots to connect.

  | Target | Aiming | Hit rate |
  | ---- | ---- | ----: |
  | <span style="color:red">A</span>BCD | 1/4 | 25% |
  | <span style="color:red">AB</span>CD | 2/4 | 55% |
  | <span style="color:red">ABC</span>D | 3/4 | 75% |
  | <span style="color:red">ABCD</span> | 4/4 | 100% |

- Points
  
  Points are awarded based on the targets you shoot down. Each shot costs 1 point.

  | | Points | <center>Example</center> |
  | --- | ---: | --- |
  | 1 Letter | 2 | <span style="color:red">AB</span>CD ⇒ 2 X 2 - 1 = 3 Points |
  | Complete | 10 | <span style="color:red">ABCD</span> ⇒ 4 x 2 - 1 + 10 = 17 Points |
  | Shot | -1 | |

  "Complete" means that all the letters are in focus.
